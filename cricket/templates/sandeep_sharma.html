IS SANDEEP SHARMA BETTER THAN BHUVNESHWAR KUMAR, JASPRIT BUMRAH AND MOHAMMED SHAMI?

INTRODUCTION

Sandeep had initially started as a batter during his school days, but his coach advised him to take up bowling, which he did and has since excelled at it. He featured in two Under-19 World Cups for India. Despite not being able to impress during the tour of Zimbabwe, Sandeep did not give up and worked hard in the domestic circuit.

On May 5 2017, at the Chinnaswamy stadium in Bengaluru, Sharma became the first bowler to dismiss Chris Gayle, Virat Kohli and AB de Villiers in the same game to lead his side to a victory in a low-scoring thriller.
